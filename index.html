Beyond the Syllabus Explained

Think of ‘Beyond the Syllabus’ as a roadmap for the journey from college into the workforce. While college provides the technical knowledge needed to earn a degree, there are other useful skills that can be developed outside of the classroom. The pages on this site are designed to follow the progression of a student’s college experience, from career exploration to professional development and workplace readiness. There are also links provided to MiraCosta’s career resource center and other helpful resources. Feel free to explore the site in any order, the world is your oyster!

Why This Matters

College graduates often think of receiving a diploma as a golden ticket to a successful career. However, after the excitement of graduation, the reality is that graduates still have to land their desired jobs and learn how to keep them. Do the years that students endure to receive a diploma teach them all there is to know about what happens after they are free of their academic obligations? Research suggests that although colleges provide valuable academic knowledge, many graduates still feel unprepared for the transition into the workforce. This indicates that while colleges play an important role in preparing students for their future careers, students must also take initiative in developing the practical and professional skills needed to successfully enter the job market.
